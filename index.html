I am an Assistant Professor of Department of Electronic Engineering (EE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I study audio / music understanding and generation. I also study general signals processing and audio-centric multiple modality models and audio large language models (LLMs)..

Before joining CUHK, I was a research scientist at ByteDance. I finished my PhD at University of Surrey, advised by Mark D. Plumbley and Wenwu Wang. I finished my graduate and undergraduate degrees at South China University of Technology, working with Qianhua He.

Open positions for RAs, PhD, Postdocs, and visitors are available. Please contact Qiuqiang Kong via email.
